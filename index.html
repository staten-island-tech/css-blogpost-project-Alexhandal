Sports Blog

Baseball

Baseball is a game in which two teams try to score as many points as they can to win. There is a team batting, and a team in the field throughout the whole game. Whatever team has the highest score at the end of nine innings wins.

Football

Football is a game that is similar to baseball in a way by having two teams trying to score as many points as they can to win. In football you play 4 fifteen minute quarters, and at the end of that whoever has the most points wins. Some examples of some football teams are the New York Giants and the New York Jets.

Basketball

Basketball is a game which has an objective of scoring the most points and beating the other team. It originated in the United States and has spread all over the world. Some New York basketball teams are the New York Knicks and the Brooklyn Nets.
